Already know what you're doing? Try these if you need a challenge!

Stand on one leg and hold the other in front of you. While holding this position have your other hand above your head or dor balance.

Start on your back and bring your legs up to the sky. Then slowly bring your knees towards you. Bring one to your face and hold for ten seconds, then switch legs. Try to aim to do five each leg.

Place one hand on the floor and try to keep your leg straight. With the other leg, bring it behind you and catch it with your open hand. Try to hold this for ten seconds and switch sides.
