If you're just getting into yoga and meditation, this is for you.

Start off with 5 minutes of your legs crossed and your eyes closed. Try to avoid thinking of anything and keeping your mind clear.

Take one leg at a time and stretch it out to the side. Feel the inside of your thigh stretching. Do this five times for each leg.

Take one arm at a time and stretch it to the other side. Feel side of your chest stretching. Do this five times each arm.
